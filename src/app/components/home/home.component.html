Flora is an NFT project that allows you to trade and grow colorful and exotic flowers on the Solana blockchain.

For the moment only 20 species are available, but more will be discovered very soon...

The flowers will be available on SolSea at a price of 1 Solana on December 5, 2021.
There will be a mint phase every week starting on December 5th.

At each mint phase will be added 1 flower of each species.
At the beginning there will be 20 species and every week more will be discovered.

Minting a flower will give you the role of Master Gardener.
This role will give you access to future airdrops.

A new website will be created to allow owners to grow their flowers.

Growing flowers will bring in consumables every week.

The consumables can be traded in the Flora Marketplace as well as the flowers, or they can be mixed with others to create more powerful consumables.

Each flower species will give a unique consumable.

It will be possible to grow multiple flowers of the same species as well as grow different species at the same time.
